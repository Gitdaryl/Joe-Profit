## HOW DIGITAL ACCESS WORKS (IMPORTANT)

When someone buys a digital product (audiobook, ebook, or bundle), access is stored in their web browser on their device - like a saved bookmark but invisible. There is no username or password to remember.

**This means:**
- Access works on the device and browser they used when they purchased
- If they switch to a different device (e.g., phone → computer), they may need help restoring access
- If they clear their browser history or cache, access may disappear
- If they use a different browser (e.g., bought on Chrome, now opening Safari), it won't work

**If they've lost access:** Instruct them to email admin@yetigroove.com with their name and the email address they used to purchase. We can restore their access.

## THE READ ALONG EXPERIENCE (How to Use It)

The Read Along is the most special - and most confusing - product. Here's what it does:

1. You see a digital book on screen (all 298 pages)
2. An audio player plays Joe reading each chapter
3. When a new audio chapter starts, the book automatically jumps to that section of the book
4. BUT you still flip the pages yourself within a chapter - the audio does NOT turn pages word-by-word

**How to get started:**
1. Go to drjoeprofit.com/read-along
2. A welcome screen will appear - press the big gold Play button to hear instructions
3. Press the Play button on the audio player at the bottom of the screen to start listening
4. Use the Prev and Next buttons below the book to flip pages, or swipe if on a phone/tablet

**Common confusion - "the book is stuck":**
If someone says the book is frozen or not moving, they are likely waiting for it to auto-flip. It doesn't - they have to flip it themselves. Explain: "The book won't turn on its own - you're in the driver's seat! Use the Prev and Next buttons at the bottom of the book to flip pages while you listen."

**Common confusion - "I'm stuck on Acknowledgments":**
The book opens to the Acknowledgments section first. This is the beginning of the book - not a problem. Just press the Next button to start moving through pages. Once you press Play on the audio, it will jump to the right chapter as you listen.

**Audio stopped / gone silent:**
If the audio suddenly goes quiet, it may have been loading a new track. Give it 10 seconds and it should resume on its own. If still silent after 15 seconds, refresh the page - your progress is saved and you won't lose your place.

**How to replay a chapter:**
On the story chapter pages, if you want to hear a chapter again - tap the back arrow once. It will scroll you to the top of that chapter so you can press Play again. If you tap the back arrow a second time quickly, it will take you to the previous chapter.

**"How do I get my book after I buy it?"**

Physical orders get a confirmation from our payment processor. Digital orders open immediately in the browser - no email needed. If they're worried the payment didn't go through, have them check their bank or card statement first, then email admin@yetigroove.com.

**"I can't find where to read/listen after I bought it."**

- eBook → drjoeprofit.com/ebook
- Bundle / Read Along → drjoeprofit.com/read-along
Important: They need to use the same device and browser they had open when they purchased.

**"I bought it on my phone but want to use my computer (or vice versa)."**
Access is tied to their original browser/device. To move it: email admin@yetigroove.com with your name and purchase email - they can set up access on a new device.

**"How do I make the text bigger?"**
In the eBook or Read Along, there is a magnifying glass (🔍) button near the bottom of the book. Tap it to zoom in on any page.

**"The audio is too fast / too slow."**
There is a speed button in the audio player - it shows 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x etc. Tap it to change the speed.

**"I lost my place / where do I pick back up?"**
Your place is saved automatically when you close the page. Just come back to the same link (audiobook, ebook, or read-along) on the same device and it should remember where you were.

**"Can I listen without internet?"**

**"Is this on Audible or Amazon Kindle?"**
The book is available in print and on Kindle through Amazon. The audiobook and Read Along experience are exclusive to the website - you won't find them on Audible.

**"I want to buy a book for someone else as a gift."**
